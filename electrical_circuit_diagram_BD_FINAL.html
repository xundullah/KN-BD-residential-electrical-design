SHEET 1 — SINGLE LINE DIAGRAM (4 Independent Single-Phase Services)

DESIGN PARAMETERS — 3 APT + COMMON, NO GEYSERS

⚡ Each apartment is electrically independent. NO geysers anywhere — toilets are simple (1 light + sockets).

  • Unit 1 (Top-Left): M.Bed + Bed-2 + Drawing/Dining + Kitchen + 2 Toilets + Verandah, 2 ACs → 5.5 kW → 32 A RCCB, sanctioned 5 kVA
  • Unit 2 (Bottom-Left): M.Bed + Bed-2 (with AC) + Drawing/Dining + Kitchen + 2 Toilets + 2 Verandahs, 3 ACs → 6.5 kW → 32 A RCCB, sanctioned 6 kVA
  • Unit 3 (L-shape duplex TR+BR): 2 × M.Bed + Small Room + Drawing/Dining + Kitchen + 2 Toilets + Verandah + Open Terrace, 3 ACs → 7.2 kW → 40 A RCCB, sanctioned 7 kVA
  • Common: Stair + Lobby + 1 HP Pump + Roof lights → 2.5 kW → 20 A RCCB, sanctioned 3 kVA

Total sanctioned load: 21 kVA across 4 meters.

Water pump on Common meter — serves all 3 units, fair billing via owners' association.

DPDC limit: 7.5 kVA per single-phase meter — all units fit within limit (Unit 3 at 7 kVA is the largest).

Earthing — 4 Separate Earth Pits (TT)

  • 25×3 mm GI strip or Cu rod, 2.5 m deep, charcoal + salt layer.
  • Target earth resistance < 1 Ω. Test before commissioning.
  • Body earth wire = 1.5 mm² Cu (green) in every circuit.
  • All metal bodies (AC outdoor unit, fridge, pump) bonded to earth bus.

IEE Wiring Regulations (BD adopted)Installation method, derating, voltage drop ≤ 4%
IEC 60364Low-voltage installation safety
Voltage drop check — worst case (Unit 3, meter to CU run ~25 m): Demand current ≈ 22 A. With 10 mm² Cu (0.011 Ω/m): drop = 22 × 25 × 0.011 × 2 = 12 V ≈ 5.2%. Marginal — recommend 16 mm² for runs over 25 m. For shorter runs (≤ 20 m), 10 mm² gives ~4% drop, within IEE limit. Verify actual route length on site.
SAFETY MANDATES:
  1. Each apartment must have its own 30 mA RCCB after the meter — BNBC requirement.
  2. Meters in locked, weather-protected cabinet, accessible only to DPDC inspectors.
  3. No tenant's wiring may share neutral with another tenant — strict isolation.
  4. Water pump must have DOL starter for inrush current handling.
  5. All work by Govt. Electrical Inspectorate (CEI) licensed electrician.
  6. Pre-energisation tests: insulation > 1 MΩ, earth continuity < 0.5 Ω, RCCB trip-time recorded.
  7. All toilet sockets must be IP44 minimum, positioned 1200 mm AFFL, outside splash zone.
  8. Terrace sockets must be IP54, with weatherproof covers.
